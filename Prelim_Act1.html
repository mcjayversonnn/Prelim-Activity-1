Images & "Something To Know" of My Top 10 Places

1. Mount-Fuji, Japan

Something to know: Mount Fuji is Japan's tallest peak and a symbol of beauty, especially stunning during cherry blossom season.

2. Seoul, South Korea

Something to know: Seoul blends modern skyscrapers with ancient palaces and is famous for its vibrant street food and K-culture.

3. Matterhorn, Switzerland

Something to know: The Matterhorn is one of the most iconic mountains in the Alps, attracting climbers and photographers worldwide.

4. Rome, Italy

Something to know: Rome is known as the Eternal City, filled with ancient ruins, Renaissance art, and delicious Italian cuisine.

5. Paris, France

Something to know: France is celebrated for its romantic cities, world-class wines, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower.

6. Boracay, Philippines

Something to know: Boracay is famous for its powdery white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, perfect for relaxation and water sports.

7. El Nido Palawan, Philippines

Something to know: El Nido is known for its dramatic limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, and rich marine biodiversity.

8. Batanes, Philippines

Something to know: Batanes offers breathtaking rolling hills, traditional stone houses, and a peaceful island atmosphere.

9. La-Union, Philippines

Something to know: La Union is a top surfing destination in the Philippines, also known for its vibrant food scene and sunsets.

10. Baguio, Philippines

Something to know: Baguio, the "Summer Capital of the Philippines," is loved for its cool climate, pine trees, and colorful flower festivals.
